Chapter One
The First Hello
It all started on November 3rd with a simple WhatsApp text. By 9:30 PM that night, a call that felt like a reunion with a soul known for lifetimes.

The magic was instant.
She ordered me Zomato lunch on day three. I was done for.
By November 16th, our parents met in Shahganj while Prajwal was in Pune. Watching her radiant happiness meeting his family was the moment the fairytale truly began.
Chapter Two
Written in the Stars
November 25th: Prajwal flew from Pune to Lucknow. Three days later, his father asked: "Are you sure?" The answer was instant: "100%."

At Starbucks, while families prepared the shagun, forty stolen minutes were more than enough. They already knew.
The Roka. The Tika. The Mithai. And a parking lot that proved the universe approved.
On November 29th, Shiwangi arrived in a beautiful Chikan Kurta. Hazelnut Factory in Hazratganj. Then Platform 1. Coach A3, Berth 29. An overnight journey to Delhi — their first as a couple, heading toward a future.
